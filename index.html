Yingzhi Zhang

I am a Ph.D. student at the Laboratory of Cognitive Systems Science, University of Tsukuba. My research work focuses on analyzing driver visual behavior and improving risk perception to support safer driving.

Previously, I worked as a research assistant at the HOIS Lab, Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where I worked on multimodal methods for driver behavior detection.

My research interests lie at the intersection of driver assistance systems, human-computer interaction, and computer vision.
